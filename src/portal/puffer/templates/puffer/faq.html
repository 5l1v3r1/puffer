Frequently Asked Questions

What is Puffer?

Puffer is a Stanford University research project aiming to improve live streaming of video over the Internet.

What channels can I watch?

Puffer rebroadcasts over-the-air television channels available in the Bay Area. Currently, we rebroadcast local affiliates of CBS, NBC, ABC, Fox, Univision, and PBS.

What platforms support Puffer?

Puffer works on most operating systems and browsers. A list of platforms on which Puffer is not supported follows:

  • Any browsers on iOS
  • Safari on macOS
  • IE on Windows
  • Samsung Internet on Android
