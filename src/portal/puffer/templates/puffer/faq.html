Frequently Asked Questions


What is Puffer?

Puffer is a Stanford University research project aiming to improve live streaming of video over the Internet. (Insert IRB info etc.)

How can I sign up?

Puffer is limiting growth to ensure we do not overwhelm our research-oriented infrastructure. To do so, we require that users possess invitation tokens in order to create new accounts. To request an invitation token visit this page. Finally, please keep in mind that Puffer is only available to users in the United States.

What channels can I watch?

Puffer rebroadcasts over-the-air television channels available in the Bay Area. Currently, we rebroadcast local affiliates of CBS, NBC, ABC, Fox, Univision, and PBS.
